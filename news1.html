Rejection for Murder of 17-year-old in Antioquia

July 16, 2021 Medellín

His family’s search for a minor ended in tragedy. The 17-year-old girl left her home in the Paris district of Bello on Tuesday, July 13, but did not return.

The next day, the dead body of the young woman was found by the authorities, who indicated that the alleged killer, a neighbor of the sector, had given himself up and confessed to the crime and the location of the minor.

The man apparently convinced the young woman to meet him at his home and murdered her.

"The boy was a local, we didn’t know him, he never showed his face or anything," a friend of the victim told Caracol News.

Tania Posada Legarda, Director for Women of the municipality of Bello and La Mesa Municipal De Erradicación De Violencias Contra Las Bellanitas, rejected this act of femicide and called on the inhabitants to immediately denounce an act of violence against women.

"We urge all women who suffer violence or know, as well as men, someone who suffers it, to raise their voices, denounce and seek help to break the cycle of this scourge. We invite you not to naturalize violence, to comply with the precept of zero tolerance, since there is no violence that is not dangerous, "Posada said.
